# Terms of Service Template

## Parties
Identify the legal entity operating the company, the customer, and the product or service.

## Service
Describe the paid offer, customer responsibilities, support limits, and any third-party services used to deliver the service.

## Fees and Billing
State price, billing cadence, payment processor, failed-payment handling, taxes, and whether renewals are automatic.

## Acceptable Use
Reference the acceptable use policy and reserve the right to suspend abusive, illegal, fraudulent, or platform-violating use.

## Disclaimers and Liability
Define warranty limits, prohibited reliance, liability cap, and exclusions that match the jurisdiction and customer type.

## Termination
Describe cancellation, suspension, data export, and post-termination obligations.

## Governing Law
Set governing law, venue, dispute process, and contact details for notices.
