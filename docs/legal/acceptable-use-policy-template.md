# Acceptable Use Policy Template

Customers may not use the service to:

- Violate laws, regulations, platform terms, or third-party rights
- Send spam, deceptive outreach, phishing, malware, or abusive content
- Generate fraudulent transactions, fake reviews, or misleading claims
- Process regulated data without written approval
- Reverse engineer, overload, scrape, or disrupt the service
- Bypass security, usage limits, billing, or approval controls

The company may suspend access, remove content, block integrations, or terminate accounts when use creates legal, security, platform, or reputational risk.
