# Legal Templates

These templates are starting points for company launch review. They are not legal advice and must be reviewed for the actual entity, jurisdiction, product, customer type, payment flow, and data practices before a company accepts money.

Required before paid launch:

- Legal owner, entity name, and jurisdiction
- Terms of service
- Privacy policy
- Refund policy
- Acceptable use policy
- Tax posture, invoice template, and bookkeeping categories
- Data processing addendum when customer personal data is processed for another business
