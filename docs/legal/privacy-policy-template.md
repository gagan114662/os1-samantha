# Privacy Policy Template

## Controller
Name the legal entity, contact address, and privacy contact.

## Data Collected
List account data, payment metadata, support messages, usage analytics, device/browser data, and customer-provided content.

## Purpose
Explain service delivery, billing, fraud prevention, support, security, analytics, legal compliance, and product improvement.

## Processors
List payment processors, hosting, analytics, email, support, AI providers, and any other vendors that receive personal data.

## Retention
Define retention periods for leads, customers, invoices, support records, logs, and deletion requests.

## Rights
Explain access, correction, deletion, portability, opt-out, and complaint paths where applicable.

## International Transfers
Document cross-border transfer mechanism when data leaves the customer's jurisdiction.
