# Refund Policy Template

## Scope
State which products, subscriptions, digital goods, services, and custom work are refundable.

## Standard Terms
Define the refund window, eligibility conditions, partial refunds, and how usage affects eligibility.

## Non-Refundable Items
List completed custom work, consumed credits, third-party pass-through costs, and abuse/fraud exceptions.

## Process
Provide the support channel, required evidence, review timeline, and payment method used for approved refunds.

## Chargebacks
Explain that customers should contact support first and that fraud or abuse may lead to account suspension.
